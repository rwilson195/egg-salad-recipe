Egg Salad

A classic protein-filled recipe to eat by itself or as a sandwich. Also Rasheena's go-to recipe.

Ingredients:

  • eggs
  • mayonaise or salad dressing
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. boil eggs
  2. peel boiled eggs
  3. mash eggs in bowl
  4. add mayo/salad dressing, salt and pepper
  5. enjoy homemade egg salad!
